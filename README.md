# American Dream — Interactive Sales Deck

Cinematic, browser-based sales storytelling designed to feel like a product experience.

---

## Overview

This is not a traditional website. It is an interactive sales tool built to replace fragmented pitch workflows with a single, immersive experience for tenants, sponsors, and event partners.

---

## ✨ Experience Highlights

- Cinematic intro experience
- Video-first storytelling
- Immersive non-linear navigation
- Interactive expandable modules
- Lottie-powered micro interactions
- Smooth motion and transitions

---

## 🧠 Product Thinking

Built as a tool, not a presentation.

The experience is designed for self-guided exploration, allowing users to move through the story at their own pace while staying anchored in business intent. Every interaction is aimed at driving meaningful actions across leasing, sponsorship, and events.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

**Frontend**

- Next.js (App Router)
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS

**Animation**

- Framer Motion
- DotLottie React

**Deployment**

- Vercel

---

## 🤖 AI Integration

AI was used to accelerate iteration across UI/UX ideation, animation direction, and interaction exploration. It acted as a creative co-pilot to speed up refinement and experimentation, not as a replacement for product thinking or design judgment.

---

## 🎨 Design Direction

Inspired by Apple for minimal UI, Tesla for product storytelling, and Disney / Universal for immersive experience design.

The goal was simple:

> To create a cinematic, non-linear interface that feels like a product, not a deck.
